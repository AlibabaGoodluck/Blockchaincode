Our General Security Practices

Company

- Employees are required to encrypt their hard drives, use strong passwords and enable screen locking.

- BitGo performs criminal background checks on all employees at time of hiring.

- No single BitGo employee has access to any keys.

- Operational keys secured in bank vaults.

Product

- Rate-limiting in the login and 2-Step Verification APIs to prevent brute-force attacks.

- Outside security firms perform regular security audits of code and infrastructure.

Operational

- 100% lockout; 24x7.

- Separation of duties for writing and pushing code.

- Split and vaulted storage of critical key material.
